    About Me

    My name is Alicia and I currently work as a graphic designer. One of my greatest passions in life has been art. I've actually been participating in art shows since I was sixteen. I've even had the opportunity to go to LA to have my art shown as well I've been featured in two indie art magazines. My favorite artists include H.R. Giger, Rembrandt, Carravagio, Théodore Géricault, Salvador Dali, Diego Rivera, Mark Mignola, and Lee Bermejo.
